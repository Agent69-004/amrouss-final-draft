Yes. The mineral pool is maintained at a comfortable 28°C in every season.

Can you arrange an airport transfer?+

Yes. Our concierge can arrange a private transfer from Fès–Saïss Airport or the Azrou station.

Is Amrouss Dar family friendly?+

Absolutely. We offer family rooms, flexible dining and gentle nature experiences for all ages.

Is the hotel accessible by car?+

Yes, with complimentary secured parking on site and an easy private approach from Azrou.
